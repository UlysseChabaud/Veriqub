The Veriqub project is funded through Horizon Europe and brings together five partners: Inria (France, coordinator), Chalmers University of Technology (Sweden), Sorbonne University (France), CNRS (France) and University of Milan (Italy). It runs from September 2023 to August 2027. Learn more.
